Create a single-page app in a single HTML file with the following requirements:
- Name: Hangman
- Goal: guess all the hidden letters in the secret word
- the UI is monochrome: black and green. The color are like a Commodore PET from 1979
- all letters that are printed are in upper case
- the top of the screen is centered and says HANGMAN
- the left of the screen is a hangman's hook
- the right are where the hidden letters are shown. Each hidden letter is shown as an underline.
- there is a box at the bottom of the screen. When letters are guessed, you see the letter in the box.
- there should be a list of 100 different words that the game uses for the player to guess. For each game, the word is chosen randomly. All words must be between 6 to 10 characters long. All words must each have at least 5 different letters. All words must be among the top 5000 most commonly used words in English. All words must use United States style spelling.
